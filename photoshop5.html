Creepy demented melty face... because yes

The last image in my display, this picture has somewhat of a special meaning for me. I created this picture during the Weekend of Many Photoshops (there were two). It's a fusion between a random stock image woman and a melted face (Reddit is a place of many wonders). Why did I create this image? I'm honestly still not entirely sure. It was mostly just to have fun, which is one of the things I really like about it. It was the moment where I was fully able to let loose and let my mind/creativity take over. Now, because this image is so strange, there was no question of even trying to make it look realistic. Instead, I just had to make the face's color match the neck's color, which was made easier by the fact that I filtered the whole image so the colors were distorted anyways. This image was also my first real introduction to the clone stamp tool, which would prove both friend and foe later in the creative process. I'm honestly really happy with how this one came out, because it's a great representation of what I've been trying to do with my Photoshops: making them weird, fun, and overall a good time.
